Top 3 Activities in San Francisco Japantown

  • Kabucki Theatre

    • Kabuki Theatre

    Kabuki theatre has a blend of traditional Japanese performance art and modern creations. It showcases Japan's theatre culture through its storytelling, acting, and costumes.

  • Benihana

    • Benihana

    Benihana is a hibachi grill, where a chef will perform Teppanyaki. A meal created before you, you can admire the architecture, go shopping, and enjoy food and drink.

  • Pagoda

    • Pagoda

    The San Francisco Peace Pagoda and Peace Plaza are central in the Japan Center Mall, and was constructed in the 1960s.It was was a modern design featuring a circular roof, unusual for a pagoda.
